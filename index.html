OUR STORY

Where Pedagogy Meets Technology

Ovdos was born from a simple observation: institutions are complex ecosystems, yet their management tools were stuck in the past. We combined the deep, frontline experience of a university educator with world-class engineering to build Anti Schedule and the future of Ovdos.

🎓
Non-Tech Founder

Vishal Bhardwaj

A respected University Professor with years of experience in the educational trenches. Vishal witnessed firsthand the cascading chaos caused by manual scheduling and teacher absenteeism. He brought the "pedagogical blueprint"—the deep understanding of classroom problems—that forms the soul of our software.

💻
Tech Founder

Vaibhav Katare

The technical architect behind Ovdos. Vaibhav translated complex educational constraints into a high-performance AI engine. His vision for "anti-gravity" scheduling—making the impossible tasks effortless—turned Vishal's pedagogical insights into the lightning-fast, conflict-free reality of Anti Schedule.
